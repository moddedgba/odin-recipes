Lasagna

Meaty, made-from-scratch tomato sauce and cheesy filling.

Ingredients

  • A pound of ground meat
  • Onion
  • A can of tomato sauce
  • A can of crushed tomatoes
  • Two tablespoons of parsley
  • One clove of garlic
  • Sugar
  • Dried basil, oregano, salt and black pepper
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cottage cheese, parmesan and shredded mozarella
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Cook the meat until browned. Add the onion and cook until it's translucent. Stir in the canned tomatoes. Add half the parsley, garlic, basil, 1.5 teaspoons of salt, oregano and sugar
  2. Boil the lasagna noodles in lightly salted water until they're al dente
  3. Mix the cheeses, eggs, the remaining parsley, the remaining salt and pepper in a bowl
  4. Layer the ingredients, starting with sauce, followed by noodles and finishing with mozzarella
  5. Cover with foil and bake for about half an hour. Then, remove the foil and bake until the top is golden brown
