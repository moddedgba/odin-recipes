Ramen

Easy to make ramen soup with ginger, soy sauce, sesame and chili oil for delicious flavor

Ingredients

  • 3 and a half cups of vegetable broth
  • 1 package of ramen noodles
  • 2 teaspoons of soy sauce
  • Half a teasopon of chili oil
  • Half a teaspoon of minced fresh ginger root
  • A teaspoon of sesame oil
  • 2 sliced green onions

Steps

  1. Combine the broth and noodles in a medium saucepan. Cover and bring to a boil over high heat, stirring to break up the noodles
  2. Reduce the heat and add soy sauce, chili oil, and ginger. Simmer while uncovered for 10 minutes
  3. Stir in sesame oil and garnish with green onions
